How We Stand Out

Blue Orange Is A Premier Digital Marketing Agency Focused On Serving The Needs Of Many Companies Seeking Greater Visibility Online Through Various Marketing Channels. Our Original Focus Was Strictly On Design And Video Services.

We Quickly Moved Into Content Marketing And Social Media. We Have Now Evolved Into A Full-Service Digital Marketing Firm Providing Expert Solutions And Integrated Resources For Clients Seeking Superior Service And Expert Advice Designed To Increase Online Visibility, Traffic, Lead Generation, And Revenue.

We Provide Highly Focused Digital Marketing Services For Companies In Competitive Markets. Our Services Include Social Media Design, Visual Identity, And Branding Design, Motion Graphic Design, Printing And Packaging Design, Animation Drawing, SEO, SEM, Email Marketing, Paid Search Management (PPC), Paid Social Media, Display, Reputation Management, Website Design, And Development, Web Hosting, And General Digital Marketing Consulting.

Our Clients Include Small And Large B2B And B2C Companies Including ECommerce And Lead Generation Websites.

Our Goal Is To Deliver Intensely Focused Digital Marketing Solutions That Exceed Expectations.

Yehya Alaa Fallaha

CEO and Founder

Yehya Fallaha is a seasoned professional with over 10 years of experience in the design, photography, video montage, and motion graphics industry. Since 2014, he has been working as a successful freelancer, credited with formulating and implementing effective marketing strategies for numerous medium and small-sized companies across various countries. With a proven track record in leading marketing and technical teams, Yehya has collaborated with several local and international organizations, companies, and institutions, including Mobaderoon, JCI, and the Aleppo Chamber of Industry. In addition to his professional accomplishments, Yehya possesses exceptional public speaking skills, which he has utilized to train and inspire Syrian youth in various fields such as design, e-marketing, and photography. He has also been a staunch supporter of youth in several pioneering projects and international events, including TEDx. In 2018, Yehya founded Blue Orange, the first startup company in Aleppo specializing in electronic marketing and website marketing. He trained and qualified a team to work alongside To this day, he continues to develop Blue Orange, renewing its goals and strategies to become the leading company in Aleppo and Syria, and who knows maybe in the world.
